## Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 2024

Image Merge Tools ("we," "our," "us," or "Company") operates the imagemergetools.com website and related services. This Privacy Policy explains our data collection, usage, and protection practices.

### 1. Information We Collect

#### Information You Provide
- **Uploaded Images**: You voluntarily upload images for processing
- **Text Input**: Any text you enter for overlays or captions
- **Contact Information**: Email addresses submitted through contact forms

#### Automatically Collected Information
- **Browser Information**: Browser type, version, and language
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, processing times
- **IP Address**: For security and analytics purposes
- **Cookies**: To improve user experience and track preferences

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- **Process your requests** - Merge images and add text as requested
- **Improve services** - Understand usage patterns and optimize tools
- **Provide support** - Respond to your inquiries
- **Security** - Prevent fraud and maintain system security
- **Analytics** - Measure performance and user engagement
- **Legal compliance** - Meet regulatory requirements

### 3. Data Retention

#### Images
- **Processing**: Images are processed immediately
- **Storage**: Images are NOT permanently stored on our servers
- **Automatic Deletion**: Images are deleted within 24 hours of processing
- **Local Processing**: Some images may be processed locally in your browser

#### User Data
- **Minimal Collection**: We collect minimal personal information
- **Retention Policy**: Data retained only as long as necessary
- **Deletion**: You can request data deletion at any time

### 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

✅ **SSL/TLS Encryption** - All data transmitted securely  
✅ **Secure Servers** - Server security and firewalls  
✅ **Access Controls** - Restricted data access  
✅ **Regular Audits** - Security assessments  
✅ **No Third-party Sharing** - Your data stays private  

### 5. User Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request what personal information we hold
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out**: Opt out of analytics and tracking

Contact us at privacy@imagemergetools.com to exercise these rights.

### 6. GDPR Compliance

We comply with GDPR for European Union residents and provide full transparency regarding data processing practices.

### 7. CCPA Compliance

California residents have specific privacy rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

### 8. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries, data subject requests, or to exercise your rights:

📧 **Email:** privacy@imagemergetools.com  
🏢 **Support:** support@imagemergetools.com
