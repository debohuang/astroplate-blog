## Getting Started with Image Merge

Our Image Merge Tool makes it easy to combine multiple images into one. Follow this guide to get started.

### Step 1: Select Your Merge Format

Choose from multiple merge options:
- **Horizontal Merge**: Place images side by side
- **Vertical Merge**: Stack images one on top of another
- **Grid Merge**: Arrange images in a perfect grid (2x2, 3x3, etc.)
- **Long Image Merge**: Perfect for social media stories and pins

### Step 2: Upload Your Images

1. Click the upload area or drag and drop your images
2. You can upload multiple images at once
3. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF

### Step 3: Customize Spacing

Adjust the gap between images:
- **No Gap**: Images touch each other
- **Small Gap**: 5px spacing
- **Medium Gap**: 10px spacing
- **Large Gap**: 20px spacing

### Step 4: Preview and Download

- Preview your merged image
- Adjust if needed
- Click "Download" to save as PNG, JPG, or WebP

### Tips for Best Results

- Use images with similar dimensions for better results
- Keep file sizes under 10MB for faster processing
- Supported maximum resolution: 8000x8000px
