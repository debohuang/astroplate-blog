## About Image Merge Tools

Welcome to Image Merge Tools, your go-to platform for free, easy-to-use image merging and text overlay solutions.

### Our Mission

We believe that powerful image editing tools should be accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skill level or budget. Our mission is to provide free, easy-to-use tools that help creators, marketers, photographers, and anyone else bring their creative vision to life.

### Our Story

Image Merge Tools was founded by a team of developers and designers who were frustrated by complicated, expensive image editing software. We wanted to create something simple, fast, and completely free. The result is a suite of tools designed specifically for common image manipulation tasks.

### What We Offer

#### Image Merge Tool
- Combine multiple images into one
- Support for horizontal, vertical, grid, and long image formats
- Customizable spacing and layout options
- Fast processing and high-quality output

#### Text to Image Tool
- Add text overlays to your images
- 50+ professional fonts to choose from
- Full positioning and styling control
- Perfect for social media graphics and more
